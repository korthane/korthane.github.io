Who We Are

Deep expertise where it matters

Korthane is a software development and consulting company specializing in building robust backend systems, high-performance tooling, and scalable infrastructure.

We bring deep expertise in Go, Rust, and modern cloud-native technologies to help businesses solve complex technical challenges. Our team has hands-on experience with high-throughput data pipelines, distributed systems, and production-grade observability stacks.

Production Focus

Every decision targets production workloads. We design for the load you'll have next year.

You Own Everything
